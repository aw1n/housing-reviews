This website allows you to anonymously read and write reviews about the letting agents operating in St Andrews.

It was born out of the frustration of our own experience with a very nasty agency, something that made us think about the why of this sad state of affairs when it comes to housing in St Andrews.

There are good landlords and agents out there but there are many nasty folks around who abuse the fact that there's a lot of demand for housing in town. Some of them even break the law by doing things the way they do, and since they know the vast majority of their tenants are students, who will not raise any legal action against them, they just keep going untouched.

We say it's enough!

It's time to do something about it, so we created this website where the people of St Andrews can voice their opinions.

Our hope is that by making public which agencies are good and which ones should be avoided, people - both tenants and landlords - will avoid headaches.

We also intend to add a section with some tips for tenants and landlords, gathered by speaking with some people who have been in the business for some time.

For now, we suggest you also visit Shelter Scotland website, and in particular their Reclaim Your Fees website, which deals with something some agents in town do or used to do: demanding "admin fees" from their tenants. Pro tip: Premierlet does this and refuse to admit it is illegal. Avoid them at all costs and if you have rented from them in the past 5 years, check the Reclaim Your Fees website because you can easily get your money back with interest. We'll even give you a hand with it.

Good luck!
